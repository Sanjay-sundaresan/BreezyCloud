Welcome to BreezyCloud archives. You can find weather records for years 2001 through 2018 in these embedded excel sheets. Every sheet contains data for one year. There are 6 tables of data in every sheet, so 6 data parameters for every year. The years are listed at the bottom of the sheet. Click to view records of the year of your choice.

*If you find that a random cell is selected, use the smaller navigation bar at the right side of the sheet, the larger bar immediately next to it, and the horizontal navigation bar at the bottom of the sheet in order to select the cell of your choice.

Use the icons in the bottom right of the excel sheet to download the data.
