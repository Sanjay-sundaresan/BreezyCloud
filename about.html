About BreezyCloud

Weather watching enables one to track hazards as a fruitful hobby, while enjoying the accuracy of various advanced tools and vast internet resources. The unpredictability of nature is a source of great suspense and excitement in its own right.

Climate data has an increasingly important role to play in the judicious management of resources, especially in a period of unpredictable weather phenomenon. Easy access to past weather records improves analytics and response to current changes, enabling a swifter and more comprehensive strategy formulation process. Graphs can easily be synthesized using the data available in a database.
This website is an attempt to improve the experience of a user who wishes to work with data in their location, complete with options to enter, retrieve, modify and delete data with all transactions optimized to maximum extent based on the concepts imbibed through the course material.
In an inceasingly data oriented environment, it is often useful to have context while making an inference, especially in a domain as mercurial as weather.Hence, the option to view recent weather trends provides the user with valuable insight. Archives provide the user with raw data, in order to suit the requirements of weather models which are intrinsically data dependent. Thought provoking facts about the finer aspects of nature have also been included for trivia-hungry users to munch on.
NOTE:It is necessary to log in to the site in order to modify, update, delete or enter data.
